## Ingredients

- 1 French baguette, sliced into ½-inch rounds
- 4 Roma tomatoes, diced
- 4 oz fresh mozzarella, diced small
- ¼ cup fresh basil, chiffonade
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for brushing
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- 1 clove garlic, halved
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Toast the bread:** Brush baguette slices with olive oil. Broil for 2-3 minutes until golden. Rub each slice with the cut side of the garlic clove while still warm.

2. **Make the topping:** Toss diced tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Let sit for 5 minutes.

3. **Assemble:** Spoon the tomato mixture onto each toasted bread slice.

4. **Serve:** Arrange on a platter and serve immediately.

## Notes

- **Storage:** The topping keeps for a few hours refrigerated. Toast and assemble right before serving so the bread stays crispy.
- **Variations:** Add prosciutto or a drizzle of honey. Swap mozzarella for burrata for a creamier version.
- **Tips:** Let the tomato mixture sit for a few minutes so the flavors meld — the salt draws out the tomato juices and makes a natural sauce.
