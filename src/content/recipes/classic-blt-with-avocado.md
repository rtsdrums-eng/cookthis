## Ingredients

- 12 slices thick-cut bacon
- 8 slices sourdough bread, toasted
- 2 ripe avocados, sliced
- 4 leaves romaine or butter lettuce
- 2 large tomatoes, sliced
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Cook the bacon:** Lay bacon in a cold skillet and cook over medium heat, flipping occasionally, until crispy — about 8-10 minutes. Drain on paper towels.

2. **Make garlic mayo:** Mix mayonnaise with minced garlic. Spread on one side of each toasted bread slice.

3. **Build the sandwiches:** Layer lettuce, tomato slices, avocado, and 3 slices of bacon on each sandwich. Season with salt and pepper.

4. **Serve:** Press gently, cut in half on the diagonal, and serve immediately.

## Notes

- **Storage:** Best eaten fresh — assemble right before serving so the bread stays crispy.
- **Variations:** Add a fried egg on top for a BLAT+E. Use turkey bacon for a lighter take.
- **Tips:** Use the best tomatoes you can find — they make or break a BLT.
