## Ingredients

- 4 cups green cabbage, shredded
- 1 cup carrots, shredded
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. **Make the dressing:** Whisk together mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, sugar, celery seed, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.

2. **Toss the slaw:** Add shredded cabbage and carrots to the dressing. Toss until everything is evenly coated.

3. **Chill:** Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes before serving to let the flavors come together.

4. **Serve:** Give it a good toss before serving. Adjust seasoning if needed.

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps in the fridge for up to 3 days. It gets a bit softer as it sits but still tastes great.
- **Variations:** Add shredded red cabbage for color, or toss in some sliced green onions. For a tangy twist, use a vinegar-based dressing instead.
- **Tips:** Use a bag of pre-shredded coleslaw mix to save time. Let the slaw chill — it's always better after resting.
