## Ingredients

- 1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Blanch the beans:** Boil green beans in salted water for 3 minutes until bright green and tender-crisp. Drain and immediately run under cold water to stop cooking.

2. **Sauté:** Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.

3. **Toss the beans:** Add green beans to the skillet. Toss in the garlic butter for 2-3 minutes until heated through and lightly charred in spots.

4. **Finish:** Remove from heat, toss with Parmesan and red pepper flakes if using. Season with salt and pepper.

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps in the fridge for 3 days. Reheat in a hot skillet to revive the texture.
- **Variations:** Add toasted slivered almonds or crispy breadcrumbs for crunch. Use frozen green beans in a pinch — just skip the blanching.
- **Tips:** Don't skip the cold water rinse after blanching — it keeps the beans bright green and snappy.
