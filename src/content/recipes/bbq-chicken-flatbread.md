## Ingredients

- 2 large naan breads or flatbreads
- 1½ cups cooked chicken, shredded
- ½ cup BBQ sauce
- 1½ cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
- ¼ small red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
- Red pepper flakes (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Preheat:** Heat oven to 425°F (220°C). Place flatbreads on a baking sheet.

2. **Toss the chicken:** Mix shredded chicken with half the BBQ sauce until coated.

3. **Assemble:** Spread remaining BBQ sauce over each flatbread. Top with mozzarella, then the sauced chicken and red onion slices.

4. **Bake:** Cook for 10-12 minutes until cheese is bubbly and edges are crisp.

5. **Finish:** Top with fresh cilantro and red pepper flakes. Slice and serve.

## Notes

- **Storage:** Best eaten fresh. Leftovers can be reheated in the oven at 375°F for 5 minutes.
- **Variations:** Swap BBQ sauce for buffalo sauce and add blue cheese crumbles. Use pita bread if you can't find naan.
- **Tips:** Rotisserie chicken is the shortcut hero here.
