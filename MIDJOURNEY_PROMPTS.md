## Tips for Best Results

1. **Re-roll freely** — If the first generation doesn't look right, hit the refresh button. You have 200 generations on the Basic plan.
2. **Upscale your favorites** — Use the U1-U4 buttons to upscale the version you like best.
3. **Consistency tip** — All prompts use `--style raw` and `--v 6.1` for a more photorealistic, less "AI-looking" result.
4. **Aspect ratio** — All set to `--ar 16:9` for landscape web images. Change to `--ar 4:3` if your card layout is squarer.
5. **Save files** — Download the upscaled images and rename them to match the filenames listed above. Place them in your project's `public/images/` folder.
